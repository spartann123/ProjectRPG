Directions

Each animations contains 8 directions, each direction is contained in the file name.

<animation name>_<direction#>_<frame#>

direction#
0 = north
1 = north east
2 = east
3 = south east
4 = south
5 = south west
6 = west
7 = north west

For example attack_0_0005 is attack facing 'north', frame 5
